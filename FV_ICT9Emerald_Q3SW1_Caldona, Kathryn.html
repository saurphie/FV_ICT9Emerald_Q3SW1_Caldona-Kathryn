Sol Ah is a graphic designer that aspires to be a web-based comic author in the future. But one thing that stands out from her personality is her love-hate relationship with cats.

One day, she brings home a cute little feline. However, this feline turns out to be something more extraordinary. His name is Hong Jo, a cat who can transform into a human.

Hong Jo eventually falls for Sol Ah but his identity crisis has become a major problem. He has to hide his human identity from Sol Ah. Despite that, he is very determined to do whatever it takes to stay with her.

This drama was adapted from a web-based comic series written by Go A Ra.

Kim Seol Ah has a puppy-like personality. She is the total opposite of a cat who loves the company of others. She views people as either as dogs or cats. Dogs are the people who have a charming and outgoing personality who aren't afraid to express themselves. Cats on the other hand, are very timid personalities who give of a mysterious aura.

She is entangled in a love triangle between Hong Jo and Lee Jae Seon, her ex-boyfriend. In the later episodes, Sol Ah creates a webtoon that reflects her life. It is a reflection of her love story and how she met the right person for her.
